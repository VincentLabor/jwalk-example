THE 2020 VIRTUAL CONFERENCE

Join the Party!

The JesusWalk Youth Conference will be available ONLINE! In the current situation of this global pandemic, due to the spread of COVID-19, we have shifted the youth conference to an online platform. Art of Worship

May 18 - May 23 || FACEBOOK LIVE

In case you missed our live stream, we hosted our annual art benefit showcase event called Art of Worship! This family-friendly event was ONLINE for the first time and all proceeds raised at Art of Worship will go directly toward COVID-19 relief efforts. When it comes to worshiping God, there are endless possibilities!

We dedicated our time and efforts to Feeding America. Through the Art of Worship online streaming event, together we raised funds to allow children, families, and seniors to sit down at the table and share a meal.
